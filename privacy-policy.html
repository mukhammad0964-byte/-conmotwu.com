Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2024 | Effective Date: January 2024

1. Introduction

Conmot Wu Studio ("we," "us," "our," "Conmot Wu," or "Studio") operates game applications and services across App Store, Google Play, and our website (www.conmotwu.com). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our platform.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile applications, games, website, and related services (collectively, the "Services").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: Name, email address, age, country/region, username, password, profile information
  • Communication Data: Messages, feedback, support requests, survey responses
  • Payment Information: Credit card data, billing address (processed through secure third-party payment processors)
  • Contact Forms: Information submitted through website contact forms

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, device model, manufacturer
  • Usage Data: Game progress, achievements, score, playtime, features used, gameplay analytics
  • Connection Information: IP address, ISP, browser type, connection speed, timestamps
  • Location Data: General location (country, city level) based on IP address or GPS (with permission)
  • Advertising Identifiers: IDFA (iOS), Google Advertising ID (Android), advertising preference signals
  • Cookies & Tracking Technologies: Session identifiers, pixel tags, web beacons, local storage data

2.3 Third-Party Data Collection

We work with advertising partners and analytics providers who may collect data through our applications:

  • Google Analytics: Demographic data, interests, behavior patterns
  • AdMob: Advertising performance, ad impressions, user engagement with ads
  • Facebook/Meta Audience Network: User interests, ad performance, engagement metrics
  • AppsFlyer: App install source, attribution data, user acquisition metrics
  • Firebase: Crash reports, performance metrics, app diagnostics
  • Adjust/Branch: Deep linking data, user journey mapping, attribution analytics

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Deliver and maintain our Services and features
  • Process transactions and send payment confirmations
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Send promotional emails and marketing communications (with consent)
  • Personalize your gaming experience and content recommendations
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve game features and performance
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security threats
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service
  • Conduct research and analytics for product development
  • Serve targeted advertising based on legitimate business interests

4.2 Advertisement Types & Data Use

4.2.1 Banner Advertisements

Data Collected: Device identifiers, advertising ID, general location, usage patterns, game session data

Purpose: Targeted ad display based on user interests and behavior. Ads shown at top or bottom of screen during gameplay without interruption.

4.2.2 Interstitial Advertisements

Data Collected: Device type, user interests, behavioral targeting data, game progression level

Purpose: Full-screen ads displayed between game sessions, level transitions, or natural gameplay breaks. Advertisers use collected data for retargeting campaigns.

4.2.3 Rewarded Video Advertisements

Data Collected: User engagement metrics, watch completion data, device information, user interest signals

Purpose: Users voluntarily watch 15-30 second video ads to earn in-game rewards. Advertisers receive detailed engagement analytics.

4.2.4 Native Advertisements

Data Collected: User interface interaction data, content preference signals, engagement metrics

Purpose: Ads designed to match game interface aesthetics. Data used for contextual and behavioral targeting.

4.3 User Advertising Preferences

You have the following options to control personalized advertising:

  • iOS Users: Enable "Limit Ad Tracking" in Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising to opt out of personalized ads
  • Android Users: Reset or opt out of ad personalization in Google Settings > Ads > Opt out of personalized ads
  • Ad Networks: Visit individual ad network privacy pages to manage preferences across their networks
  • App Settings: Many of our games include in-app privacy settings to control targeted advertising

5. Children's Online Privacy Protection (COPPA)

5.1 Age Restrictions & Compliance

We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar laws in other jurisdictions. COPPA protects children under 13 years of age in the United States and similar regulations protect children globally.

5.2 Our Approach to Children Under 13

  • We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent
  • For games directed at children, we limit data collection to game analytics only
  • No third-party behavioral tracking is enabled for child accounts
  • Advertising in games for children is limited to non-targeted, contextual ads only
  • No persistent user identifiers are used for children's accounts without parental consent
  • Parent/Guardian contact information may be collected for consent verification only

5.3 Parental Controls & Verification

Parents/guardians can:

  • Request verification of our privacy practices for their child's account
  • Review what information we have collected about their child
  • Request deletion of their child's account and all associated data
  • Opt-in to limited data collection for in-game purchases
  • Contact us at mukhammad0964@icloud.com with subject line "COPPA Request"

5.4 Safe Gameplay Environment

We maintain:

  • Content warnings and age-appropriate game classifications
  • No external social sharing by default for child accounts
  • Restricted in-game purchase capabilities for minors
  • No collection of location data from children without parental consent
  • Regular content reviews to ensure age-appropriateness

6. GDPR Compliance (European Union & EEA)

6.1 Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data under the following lawful bases:

  • Contractual Necessity: Data required to provide gaming services and fulfill user agreements
  • Consent: Explicit opt-in consent for marketing communications and behavioral advertising
  • Legitimate Interests: Analytics, fraud prevention, game improvement based on balanced assessment
  • Legal Obligation: Compliance with applicable laws, tax requirements, and legal processes

6.2 User Rights Under GDPR

EU/EEA residents have the following rights regarding their personal data:

  • Right of Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of personal data ("Right to be Forgotten") under certain conditions
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we process your personal data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing for marketing, analytics, or profiling
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw previous consent at any time without penalty

6.3 Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary:

  • Active Account Data: Retained while account is active plus 12 months after deletion request
  • Analytics Data: Retained for 14 months for service improvement purposes
  • Advertising Data: Retained per individual ad network policies (typically 30-90 days)
  • Transaction Records: Retained for 7 years for tax and legal compliance
  • Security Logs: Retained for 90 days for fraud detection

6.4 Data Transfer Mechanisms

For data transfers outside the EEA, we implement:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) with data processors
  • Data Processing Agreements compliant with GDPR Article 28
  • Adequacy decisions where applicable
  • Appropriate safeguards for all international data transfers

7. California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) Compliance

7.1 Consumer Rights Under CCPA

California residents have the following rights regarding their personal information:

Right to Know

Right to Delete

Request deletion of personal information we have collected from you, with limited exceptions for legal compliance.

Right to Opt-Out

Opt out of "selling" or "sharing" of personal information for targeted advertising (as defined under CCPA/CPRA).

Right to Correct

Request correction of inaccurate personal information we maintain about you.

Right to Limit Use
