Ad Format Details

🎬 Rewarded Video Ads

Players watch short video advertisements in exchange for in-game rewards. Highest engagement rates and CPM. Typically 15-30 second skippable or non-skippable videos.

📱 Interstitial Ads

Full-screen advertisements displayed between game sessions. High-impact visual and video content. Shows at natural game transition points like level completion.

🏷️ Banner Ads

Non-intrusive ads displayed at top or bottom of screen during gameplay. Continuous presence with low impact on user experience. Steady revenue stream.

🎨 Native Ads

Ads designed to match game aesthetic and UI. Seamlessly integrated into user interface. Highest user acceptance and lowest negative impact on retention.

📲 App Install Ads

Promote other mobile applications through your game. Build cross-promotion opportunities and earn from user installs. Excellent partnership revenue channel.

🎯 Splash Ads

Full-screen advertisements shown during game startup or loading screens. Optimal placement before gameplay begins. Doesn't interrupt active gameplay.
